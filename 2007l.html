2007 ROBLOX Places

This page contains a multitude of ROBLOX places that were created around late 2007 through a place-stealing method that involved the names of thumbnails.
And while this lead to a lot of place copying during its time, this oversight has allowed a lot of places to be preserved — from a time before place versions!

  • Master Package - An updating zip of a lot of organized 2007 places&models.
  • HSORT - A python script I use to grab & organize archived pages for 2007 places&models. WARNING: Python experience recommended.
  • Worker's ROBLOX Hash Archive - an unsorted archive of every downloadable (late 2007) hash. WARNING: 148GB uncompressed — one single folder. Use VoidTool's Everything to search through the folder.
